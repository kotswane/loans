TERMS AND CONDITIONS FOR SMALL CREDIT AGREEMENT
("Agreement")
  1. This Agreement is made up of the Pre-Agreement Statement and Quotation, the Quotation and Loan Offer Slip and these terms and conditions. In terms of this Agreement, the Credit Provider agrees to lend me the amount set out on the Quotation and Loan Offer Slip and I agree to repay the Credit Provider the amount set out under Total Repayment of the Quotation and Loan Offer Slip.

  2. Early settlement and early repayments: I may settle this Agreement early at any time in terms of sections 122 and 125 of the National Credit Act, 34 of 2005 ("the Act") by paying the unpaid balance, the unpaid interest and all other fees and charges up to the date of settlement. I may also make early repayments under this Agreement.

  3. Addresses for receiving documents: I nominate the address set out in the Pre-Agreement Statement and Quotation for purposes of communication, including legal notices and court orders. Either party to this Agreement may change its address by delivering to the other party a written notice of the new address by hand, registered mail, or electronic mail.

  4. Payments under the Agreement: I authorise the Credit Provider to deduct the monthly instalments and other amounts due in terms of this Agreement from my bank account.

  5. Penalty clause and Legal Costs: If I default under this Agreement, I agree that the Credit Provider may charge me penalty interest on arrear instalments at the same interest rate set out in the Quotation and Loan Offer Slip. I agree that all attorneys' or registered debt collectors' costs can be recovered from me on the higher of the attorney and client scale or the published tariff recoverable by registered debt collectors.

  6. Credit Provider's right to terminate this Agreement: The Credit Provider may enforce and/or terminate this Agreement against me if I default with any of the agreed instalments in terms of this Agreement, in terms of sections 123, 129 and 130 of the Act, which may result in a court enforcing the repayment of my outstanding obligations in terms of this Agreement and a judgment against me being recorded at credit bureaus, which will be available to other credit providers.

  7. Account statements: The Credit Provider will provide a free statement to me at the end of every third month and statements during intermediate months will be provided to me on request at a cost computed at the maximum allowable provided for in the Act.

  8. Default Administration and collection charges: If I default under this Agreement, I will be liable for default and/or collection charges and reasonable and necessary expenses incurred to deliver such letter, at the maximum allowable rate allowed in law.

  9. Agreement provided and available: I was given a free copy of this Agreement when I signed it and a copy of the Act and Regulations is available from branches of the Credit Provider.

  10. Conditions, rights and obligations: This is the full agreement between the parties and there are no unwritten terms or conditions.

  11. Dispute resolution: I agree that in the event of any dispute or uncertainty, I will let Credit Provider know in writing, and if the dispute is not resolved, I may take the matter up with the National Credit Regulator at toll share number 0860 100 406.

  12. Administration order: I declare that I am not under administration, nor am I going to apply for an administration order. If I am placed under administration after entering into this Agreement, I agree that all instalments still due after the administration order is granted, fall within the ambit of Section 74 A (2)(e)(ii) of the Magistrates Court Act and are excluded in terms of Section 74 C (2) of the said act and the Credit Provider will be able to recover all moneys still outstanding in terms of this Agreement by legal action.

  13. Debt counselling: I may apply to a debt counsellor to be declared over indebted in terms of section 86 of the Act. I can access the names of debt counsellors at www.ncr.org.za.


I declare and warrant that I:
